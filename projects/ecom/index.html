Ecom

Project Type

Company Project

Project Dates

February 2013 to January 2014

Project Description

Ecom is an e-commerce Wordpress plugin that integrates with online services like Amazon, Ebay, Flickr, and Freebase. It also comes with a Wordpress theme which will be used for its product searching functionalities.

It’s main features are:

  • automatic publishing of posts with the content being the product details of the the products being sold by the client

  • Searching of products with sorting and filtering based on product popularity, customer reviews, price range and product brand
